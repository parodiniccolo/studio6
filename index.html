About the studio

A bright, versatile space for photographers and creatives in the heart of Berlin.

Studio 6 is a 48 sqm shared photography studio on Kopernicker Str. 31, about 5 minutes walk from Ostbahnhof. The space features beautiful south-facing natural light from a row of large windows, a main shooting area with multiple backdrops, gear storage, a large work table on wheels, and a small lounge/work zone. Part of the Culterim building, the studio has 24/7 access, an elevator, multiple bathrooms, and a basic kitchen.
